Vision simulation

The ADALS 2026 Call for Speakers page under simulated vision conditions, captured with CSS / SVG filters over the live page.

The page conveys meaning by lightness contrast (Navy vs Off-White, near-black text), not by hue — so every colour-deficiency view below loses no information (see achromatopsia, fully legible with zero colour). The AMD central scotoma is a visual-field limit, not a design defect.
